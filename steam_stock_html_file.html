How does virtual trading work? +
Our platform simulates real stock market mechanics using Steam games as tradable assets. You receive virtual currency to buy and sell games based on market trends, community interest, and algorithmic pricing models.
Do I need to own games to trade them? +
No, you can trade any Steam game regardless of ownership. The platform provides virtual currency and access to the entire Steam catalog for trading purposes.
How are rewards distributed? +
Weekly leaderboard competitions award gift cards to top 10 performers. Additional rewards are available through battle pass progression and special trading challenges.

About

Steam Stock Exchange bridges gaming and finance, creating an entirely new form of interactive entertainment. We transform your Steam library into an active trading portfolio where strategy, market knowledge, and quick decision-making determine success.

Our platform offers a risk-free environment to learn trading principles while competing with friends and fellow gamers. Experience the thrill of market dynamics without financial risk, earning real rewards through virtual achievement.
